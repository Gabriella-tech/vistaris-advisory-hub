Bayo Babatunde

Managing Director

Bayo Babatunde is a seasoned business strategist, enterprise development expert, and execution-focused consultant with over 15 years of cross-sector experience spanning business growth leadership, capacity building, and project exection. His work is defined by a strong execution bias, operating at the intersection of strategy, systems, and sustainability to ensure that ideas, policies, and development programs translate into measurable outcomes and long-term impact. through Vistaris, he has led the design and implementation of structured advisory frameworks, enterprise development programs, and incubation systems that enable businesses to scale efficiently within Nigeria’s evolving economic landscape.

Bayo holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Obafemi Awolowo University and an MBA from the University of the People, Colorado. He has further strengthened his expertise through executive education and certifications from globally recognized institutions, including Harvard Business School, London Business School, Yale University, and Imperial College London, with focus areas in leadership, innovation, business analysis, and coaching. He is a Certified Management Consultant (CMC), Fellow of the Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC), Certified Trainer, Facilitator & Coach (TFC), Project Management Professional (PMP), and a SMEDAN/EDC-Certified Business Development Service Provider (BDSP). These credentials reflect a strong foundation in both strategic thinking and practical delivery. Over the years, Bayo has designed and delivered large-scale capacity-building and enterprise development initiatives across Nigeria, contributing to national and international programs such as the GIZ ICSS Modular Program, the EDC–Mastercard Foundation Transforming Nigerian Youth (TNY) Project, and SMEDAN’s National MSME Capacity Building Initiatives. He has trained and mentored over 5,000 entrepreneurs, professionals, and youth across diverse sectors, equipping them with the tools to build resilient and growth-oriented enterprises. His experience also extends into digital transformation and systems deployment, where he has led the implementation of technology-driven solutions, including large-scale education management systems and digital literacy initiatives that enable organizations and individuals to participate effectively in the digital economy. Prior to founding Vistaris Advisory Hub, Bayo held senior leadership roles at Evertop Realties, Accello Limited, and Total Risk Managers, where he developed deep expertise in business strategy, operations, and startup incubation. As a facilitator and coach, he is known for a practical, engaging, and results-driven approach, translating complex concepts into actionable strategies that drive real outcomes. Beyond his professional work, he is deeply committed to advancing entrepreneurship as a tool for national development, youth empowerment, and economic resilience. Bayo continues to inspire and support a new generation of entrepreneurs and leaders across Africa to build structured, sustainable, and impact-driven businesses, anchored on the principle that strategy must lead to execution, and execution must deliver measurable results.

Olaoluwa Bamigboye (PhD)

Head, Programs & Enterprise Development

Dr. Olaoluwa Bamigboye is an agribusiness expert, enterprise development specialist, and execution-driven consultant with deep expe rience across agricultural value chains, MSME development, and rural enterprise systems. He brings a strong blend of technical expertise, business strategy, and field-level execution in designing and scaling sustainable agribusiness models.

He holds a PhD in International Agribusiness Management, complemented by a Master’s degree in Animal Production and Management and a Bachelor’s degree in Animal Science. His academic and professional journey reflects a strong commitment to advancing commercially viable, resilient, and inclusive agricultural systems. Dr. Bamigboye has led and supported multiple high-impact agribusiness and enterprise development initiatives across Nigeria and beyond. His experience spans technical and operational due diligence, value chain development, livestock production systems, and agribusiness advisory for both private sector clients and development programs. He has worked with organizations such as Farrelly & Mitchell (Ghana), Farmcrowdy, and various agricultural enterprises, delivering solutions that improve efficiency, profitability, and scalability. He has also played a key role in training, mentoring, and youth development, facilitating and supporting hundreds of agripreneurs through initiatives such as the Tony Elumelu Foundation, Leap Africa, and other national and international programs. His approach focuses on translating agricultural knowledge into viable business models, equipping participants to build sustainable and market-driven enterprises. At Vistaris Advisory Hub, Dr. Bamigboye leads the design and execution of enterprise development programs. He oversees program implementation, sector interventions, and field operations, ensuring that advisory frameworks translate into tangible economic outcomes for entrepreneurs, cooperatives, and stakeholders. His professional experience includes leading initiatives such as the development of circular livestock feed systems, large-scale livestock sourcing and trading operations, and the establishment of integrated farm models linking production to market. He has also designed sustainable procurement systems that connect smallholder farmers to structured markets, improving productivity and income levels. Dr. Bamigboye is a Certified Business Development Service Provider (BDSP) and has participated in several international and local capacity-building programs, including Erasmus+ (EU-certified), ISO 22000 Food Safety Management Systems training, and agribusiness scale-up programs. He is passionate about leveraging agriculture as a tool for economic transformation, youth empowerment, and food system resilience. His work is focused on building structured, scalable, and impact-driven agribusiness enterprises that contribute to sustainable development across Nigeria and Africa.
